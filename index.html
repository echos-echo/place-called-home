Welcome to New York City

the city that never sleeps

I'm sure that when one hears "New York City," they first associate it with Times Square: bright lights, city night, with bustling and crowded streets that seem near impossible to navigate through. And it certainly is true that to those unfamiliar with this urban landscape, traversing it may be intimidating to say the least.

This is a New York City guide that will let you experience the city the right way, more like a local, rather than as a tourist getting dragged here and there to go on pricey tours and walks. Here, you'll get the chance to read about less-frequented food places, and the best parts of the city to relax.

Complete with a short history of the big city, this site offers a compilation of just a few of the city's most charming points, as well as tips on how to travel around the busy streets.

The Quick Guide to Central Park

Photo by Sergio Souza

You may think that having an entire page dedicated to Central Park may be a bit excessive, but do keep an open mind that Central Park physically amasses 6% of the entirety of Manhattan. It is the most frequented urban park in the country, and it's all thanks to the expert landscaping, meticulous upkeep, and variety of activities. Do be mindful that Central Park is a massive piece of land, and it can be easy to get lost if you do not stay aware of your surroundings.
